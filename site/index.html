The Chattahoochee Gay-Strait Alliance is a club that acts as a support group for members of the LGBT+ community. Contrary to many people's understanding, the Chattahoochee Gay-Strait Alliance members are solely not comprised of people that identify within the LGBT+ community but also by those who do not. To elaborate, the club consists by people who identify as gay, strait, pansexual, asexual, transexual/transgender, and any other identificcation out there. If you do not know what some of those terms mean or are unfamiliar with them please visit our Flags & Orientations page.
